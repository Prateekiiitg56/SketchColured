WHAT IS SKETCHCOLURED?

SketchColured uses a custom Conditional GAN (Generative Adversarial Network) with an Attention U-Net generator, trained on over 400 epochs of anime artwork to automatically colorize your line art. The output is then super-resolved to 4× resolution using Real-ESRGAN for stunning, high-quality results.

03 THE TECH

HOW IT
WORKS

01

ATTENTION U-NET

Our deep encoder-decoder generator uses attention gates at every skip connection to precisely focus on sketch lines and boundaries.

02

PATCHGAN DISC.

A PatchGAN discriminator with spectral normalization evaluates local patches across the image to enforce realistic color textures.

03

REAL-ESRGAN

The 256×256 output is upscaled to 1024×1024 with Real-ESRGAN anime weights, producing crystal-clear, print-ready artwork.

04

COLOR TRANSFER

Optionally apply the Reinhard histogram color transfer algorithm to shift the color palette to match any reference image.
